PUBLIC ADMINISTRATION

Courses A Bachelor of Arts in Public Administration focuses on building knowledge of public administration through academic and applied perspectives. The curriculum aims to develop skills and attitudes to respond to the complexity of public administration.

  • Job opportunities
    • Some jobs in public administration include: Human resources officer,Team leader,Principal,Senior program management specialist and Chief of party
  • Entry requirements
    • Entry requirements for a Bachelor of Arts in Public Administration may include: Passing Grade 12 or Form V with a minimum credit in at least five subjects including English Language
    An 'O' Level certificate from a recognized Examination Board with a minimum grade C in at least five subjects including English Language
  • Universities that offer the course
    • Some universities that offer a Bachelor of Arts in Public Administration include: Chalimbana,ZCAS University and The University of Zambia also offers a Master of Public Administration.
