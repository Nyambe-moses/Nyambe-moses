ELECTRICAL AND ELECTRONIC ENGINEERING


Electrical and electronic engineering is a field that involves the study of electrical energy generation, transmission, and distribution. It also includes the application of electrical circuits and electromagnetism to solve problems in power systems. Some courses in this field include: Engineering Electromagnetics,Digital Signal Processing,Electrical Instrumentation,Electronic Engineering III,Communications Principles,Microcontroller Technology and Embedded Systems and Engineering Management & Entrepreneurship
  • JOB OPPORTUNITIES
    • Some job opportunities for electrical and electronic engineering graduates include: Digital Electronics Design Engineer,Graduate Antenna Engineer,Graduate Electrical Design Engineer,Group Head of Engineering and Quality,project Leader,Research Engineer,Embedded Systems
  • ENTRY REQUIREMENTS
    • Entry requirements for electrical and electronic engineering programs may include: Completing 12 years of primary and high school education NOTE Achieving a minimum score on an English test like the IELTS or TOEFL Undergoing an interview Achieving a minimum aggregate on a secondary education certificate Having an artisan or craft certificate in a relevant program
  • Universities and colleges
    • Some universities and colleges that offer electrical and electronic engineering courses include: UNZA, USM, CMRIT, University of Johannesburg, Sandip Foundation, and Coventry University.
