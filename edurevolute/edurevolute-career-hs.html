HEALTH SCIENCES

Courses Chainama College of Health Sciences offers a variety of health science courses, including diplomas, post-basic diplomas, ordinary diplomas, and certificate programs. Some of the courses offered include: Diploma in Clinical Medical Sciences General,Diploma in Environmental Health Sciences,Diploma in Registered Mental Health Nursing,Diploma in Optometry,Advanced Diploma in General Medicine Advanced Diploma in Clinical Ophthalmology

  • Job opportunities
    • Some careers in health science include: Doctors,Physicians assistants,Nurse practitioners,Physical therapists,Nurses,Pharmacists,Psychologists and counselors,Medical assistants ,Radiology technologists and MRI or CT technicians
  • Entry requirements
    • Entry requirements for health science courses in Zambia include a full Grade 12 School Certificate or GCE with 5 credits or better. Four of the credits must be in English Language, Mathematics, Biology, and Science.
  • Universities and colleges
    • The University of Zambia offers a Bachelor of Science in Clinical Medicine for Clinical Officers. Chainama College of Health
