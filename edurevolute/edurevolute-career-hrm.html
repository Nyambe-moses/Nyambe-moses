HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

A Bachelor of Human Resource Management (HRM) degree prepares students for careers in human resources in business, government, and non-profit organizations. Some courses that may be included in the program are:
Year 1 Basic economics, principles of accounts, communication, quantitative methods, principles of management, and principles of law
Year 2 Labor economics, cost management accounting, information technology, personnel management, HRM I, and introduction to industrial relations
Year 3 Financial management, industrial psychology, information technology and communication, HRM II, industrial relations, and labor law
Year 4 Research project, consulting in HRM and development, managing change, organizational behavior, HRM III, and HRM development II Some universities and colleges in Zambia that offer a Bachelor of Human Resource Management degree include: The University of Africa Prepares students for professional careers in human resources
