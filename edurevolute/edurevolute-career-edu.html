EDUCATION

Courses The University of Zambia (UNZA) offers a variety of education courses, including: Bachelor of Adult Education,Bachelor of Agricultural Science with Education,Bachelor of Arts with Education Bachelor of Arts with Education in Zambian Cultures and Ceremonies,Bachelor of Arts with Library and information Studies.

  • Entry requirements
    • UNZA's general entry requirements for undergraduates include: Grade 6 or better in English Language and O' level Mathematics Grade 6 or better in Biology, Chemistry and Physics Or Grade 6 or better in Science, Biology (or Agricultural Science) and any other acceptable subject
  • Job opportunities
    • Education beyond secondary school can lead to higher pay in the public sector and in private sectors like mining and manufacturing. Zambia's education system is structured as 7 years of primary school, 2 years of junior secondary school, 3 years of higher secondary school, and 4 years of university for undergraduate degrees.
