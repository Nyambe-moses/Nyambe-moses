LAW

History The School of Law is one of the oldest and most prestigious law schools in Zambia. It has educated many of the country's prominent lawyers, both in government and the private sector.

  • Programs
    • The school offers a Bachelor of Laws (LLB) program, which takes four years to complete. The first year is spent in the School of Humanities and Social Sciences, and the remaining three years are spent in the School of Law. The school also offers a Master of Laws (LLM) program, Postgraduate Diplomas in International Law and Human Rights Law, a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Law, and a Post Doctorate program in Law.
  • Requirements
    • To be eligible for the LLB program, you need to meet one of the following requirements: A full Grade Twelve (12) certificate with five (5) 'O' level passes with Credit or better Three (3) Cambridge 'A' level passes, including English A first degree in any field of study from a recognized University or institution A professional qualification that is academically equivalent to a first degree.
  • Post-graduate training
    • To practice law in Zambia, you need to complete a period of post-graduate practical training at the Zambia Institute of Advanced Legal Education.
