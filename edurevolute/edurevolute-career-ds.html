DEVELOPMENT STUDIES

Courses The Master of Arts in Development Studies and Diplomacy from UNITAR is an interdisciplinary program that covers development studies, sustainability, and diplomacy. The Master of Development Studies (MDS) from Cavendish University Zambia helps students develop skills like critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation.

  • Job opportunities
    • Development studies graduates can work in government, banks, and non-governmental organizations (NGOs). In government, they may work in planning and administration. In banks, they may work as loan officers, credit officers, or bank tellers. Development specialists may use computer applications like SPSS and Excel for statistical analysis.
  • Entry requirements
    • The Bachelor of Development Studies at UMP requires students to have the following entrance requirements: Mathematics level 2 or Math Literacy level 3 English level 4 Geography/History and one other Social or Commercial Sciences Subject at level 4
  • Universities that offer development studies
    • The University of Zambia's Department of Development Studies is one of the largest departments in the School of Humanities and Social Sciences. The University of Sussex in the UK is considered the best university in the world for development studies. Development studies is a field of study that covers a wide range of concerns, including poverty, environmental sustainability, climate change, and more.
